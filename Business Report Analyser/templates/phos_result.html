The volcano plot above depicts the -log10 p-value vs the log2 fold change of each phosphosite and is representative of all phosphosites from the given dataset. Phosphosites are deemed significantly up- or down-regulated if they have a p-value less than or equal to 0.05, with a log2 fold-change greater than 1.5 (upregulated) or less than -1.5 (downregulated) after exposure to the inhibitor in question. Significant fold change values are represented by the red dots, whereas non-significant ones are displayed in blue.
